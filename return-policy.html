TalesGalore

At TalesGalore, every book is chosen, packed, and sent out with immense care and love. We are a small, independent business built around stories, and like the characters in the books we cherish, we grow gently and thoughtfully — one page at a time.
🌿

Why We're Unable to Offer Returns

As a small-scale business, each order matters deeply to us. Unlike large retailers, we do not have the infrastructure to absorb the financial and environmental costs associated with returns.

In fact, frequent returns are one of the leading reasons small businesses struggle to survive. Shipping costs, reverse logistics, handling, and inventory losses can significantly impact operations at our scale.

For this reason, we are unable to accept returns or exchanges once an order has been delivered.

We understand this may be different from what you're used to, and we truly appreciate your understanding and support. By shopping with TalesGalore, you're directly helping a small business continue to share meaningful stories with families.

📖

Please Choose Thoughtfully

To help you make the right choice:

  • We provide detailed book descriptions.
  • Age recommendations are clearly mentioned.
  • Themes and formats are specified wherever possible.

If you're unsure about a book's suitability, feel free to reach out before placing your order — we are always happy to guide you.

📦

In Case of Damage

If your order arrives damaged or incorrect:

  • Please contact us within 48 hours of delivery.
  • Share clear photographs of the issue.
  • We will review the case and work toward a fair resolution.

Your thoughtful purchases help us continue curating beautiful books for little readers across homes and classrooms. Thank you for understanding that protecting our small business allows us to keep bringing stories to life.

Our Story

TalesGalore

By naming TalesGalore what it is, we would like to tell our own story of bringing up two inquisitive kids. As parents we must always have a tale ready — to channelize their energies in the right direction, to teach them life values, to calm them down, to bring a smile to their faces, to put them to sleep. The essence lies in gaiety, abundance and fulfilment. We enjoy serendipitous selection of books for our kids and through this venture, we would like to pass on the same joy of finding that one book you didn't know you were looking for. So dive in and start your treasure hunt!!
🌿

Our Philosophy

In this fast-moving era of gadgets, AI and social media, we want to slow down and embrace simplicity. At TalesGalore, we believe that childhood is built on stories. The giggles over silly characters, the wide-eyed wonder at magical worlds — these are the moments that shape imagination and nurture lifelong readers.

Every child deserves access to beautiful stories that spark curiosity, empathy and joy — and that's what we are here for. Stories don't just entertain; they provide a medium to create bonds and help children see themselves — and others — in novel ways.

📖
